Welcome!

WeCanHearYou is an open source, cross platform and free to use customer feedback system for companies and products.

Current stage

We're currently bootstraping the application, so there isn’t much to see yet. You can keep track of its progress using our GitHub repository.

Features

  • Customer-First experience: We believe that an easy to use and clean feedback platform is the way to go to enable your customers to shares its ideas with you.
  • One click log in with OAuth and Social Login. Registration is only a single click, no forms, no question, just click it!
  • Let your users share ideas on how to make your product even better. They use your products everyday and surely have tons of ideas on how you can make your products even better. Give them the voice they need, learn from them, interact, deliver and profit!
  • The best ideas come from multiple minds working together. Let your community contribute by improving existing ideas and you'll be surprised with what they can do together.
  • Hundreds of ideas require organization. You can do so by tagging ideas to make it easier to find, group and decide.
  • Share private ideas within your team: Got an idea that is not yet ready to become public? Don't worry, we've got your back. Make it private and keep shaping it untill it's ready to become public, or keep it private forever, you decide.
  • Lightning fast. Because yes, performance IS a feature. We think about performance in every aspect and take it very seriously. It makes our users happier and our servers healthier.

Demo environment

Our staging environment is hosted by Heroku and it’s automatically deployed upon commit to master.

* Demo: http://demo.canhearyou.com

How to install it

No instructions are available yet, but you'll need:

* A Linux/Windows server. There is no runtime dependency.

* A PostgreSQL database.

We also have Docker as a first-class citizen to make self-hosted deployment a lot easier. Up to date images can be found on https://hub.docker.com/r/wecanhearyou/
