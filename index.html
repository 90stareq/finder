Features

Customer-First Experience

We believe that an easy to use and clean feedback platform is the way to go to enable your customers to shares its ideas with you

Lightning fast. Because yes, performance IS a feature. We think about performance in every aspect and take it very seriously. It makes our users happier and our servers healthier.

Let your users share ideas on how to make your product even better. They use your products everyday and surely have tons of ideas on how you can make your products even better. Give them the voice they need, learn from them, interact, deliver and profit!

One click log in via social login. No forms. No hurdles. Just click it!

The best ideas come from multiple minds working together. Let your community contribute by improving existing ideas and you'll be surprised with what they can do together.

Hundreds of ideas require organization. You can do so by tagging ideas to make it easier to find, group and decide.

Share private ideas within your team: Got an idea that is not yet ready to become public? Don't worry, we've got your back. Make it private and keep shaping it untill it's ready to become public, or keep it private forever, you decide.
